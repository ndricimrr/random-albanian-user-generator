Privacy Policy

This website is an open-source attempt at providing developers with a simple-to-use user interface for generating random Albanian user data, including customized names, surnames, street names, and phone numbers. Additionally, random images are generated to depict people of different ages, aiming to provide the closest-to-real-life data for developers. The app is also used for generating names for fun purposes.

We do not collect any user data: No tracking, no data collection. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to make it clear that we do not store or track any user information.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at ndricimrr@gmail.com .

This privacy policy is subject to change without notice. Please check back periodically for any updates.
